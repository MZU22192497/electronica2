RTX 4080

This architecture is based on a 4nm process and features fourth-generation Turing cores for AI acceleration and third-generation RT cores for, well, ray tracing. The RTX 4080 Super itself features 80 Streaming Multiprocessors (SMs) combining for a total of 10,240 CUDA cores, 320 Tensor cores and 80 RT cores.31
